SWOT Analysis

Internal Strengths Weaknesses
  • Hibachi and Sushi bar
    • Have a section about them on one of the pages.
    • Have photos of fresh sushi.
    • Provide sushi coupons to encourage customers to buy sushi.
  • Healthy options
    • Have a little leaf icon on food items that are healthy.
    • Promote the vegan food offered.
    • Have a page that has all healthy options in one place.
  • Accommodates large parties
    • Quote a Yelp review.
    • Show pictures of the space.
    • Add family portions to the menu for delivery.
  • Website
    • Make it responsive.
    • Make it accessible.
    • Better design and navigation.
  • No delivery
    • Offer online ordering.
    • Have a pop up about delivery since it will be new.
    • Have a button next to menu items to add to shopping cart.
  • Specials not up to date
    • Update specials regularly.
    • Drill down the amount of specials.
    • Add a specials page.
External Opportunities Threats
  • Right next to shopping mall/highways
    • Have a “location” page with the restaurant’s and nearby shopping mall’s location listed.
    • Have photos of the restaurant.
    • Have google map showing closeness to the mall.
  • Yelp/Facebook reviews
    • Show testimonials from there on homepage.
    • Link to our page on Yelp/Facebook.
    • Show our Yelp/Facebook star ratings.
  • Holidays
    • Have holiday hours posted.
    • Have special holiday deals.
    • Have pictures of special holiday items on the menu.
  • Coronavirus
    • Emphasis cleanliness of the restaurant.
    • Testimonials on site about how clean it is.
    • Have health inspection details on site.
  • Rent being raised
    • The new website will attract more customers to offset the cost of higher rent.
    • Add online delivery to increase revenue.
    • Once the website is prettier, it can be shared on social media to bring more people in.
  • Diet Trends
    • Have a healthy options page.
    • Have nutrition information on the website.
    • Have a symbol on menu items that are healthy choices.
